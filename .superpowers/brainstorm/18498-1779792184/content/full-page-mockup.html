FAQ

Common questions

How is Sydon AI different from Helium 10 or Keepa?

Helium 10 and Keepa are research tools — they surface data and wait for you to act. Sydon AI is an action platform: agents monitor, decide, and execute on your behalf, with your approval.

How quickly will I see results?

Most sellers see measurable impact within the first 2 weeks. PPC optimization and account protection begin working immediately after your first catalog scan.

What data does Sydon AI access?

Only the business data you see in your Amazon Seller Central Dashboard via the SP-API. No personal information, no unapproved listing changes.

Can I use Sydon AI alongside my existing tools?

Yes. Sydon AI integrates with QuickBooks, HubSpot, Klaviyo, ShipStation, and more. You don't need to replace your current stack.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is yours. Export everything before cancellation — we provide a full data export on any plan.

Is Sydon AI suitable for smaller sellers?

Absolutely. The free Account Guardian plan is designed for sellers at every stage. Individual plan scales from $5K to $5M+ monthly revenue.
