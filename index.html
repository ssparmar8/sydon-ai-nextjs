05 · FAQ

The honest answers.

How is this different from Helium 10 + Keepa + QuickBooks together?
Those tools don't talk to each other. You see seven dashboards, copy numbers between them, and still miss the signal. Sydon's agents share one intelligence layer — so when Account Guardian sees a policy risk, the Listing Builder doesn't ship a change that makes it worse.
How quickly will I see results?
Account Guardian starts scanning the moment you connect — most sellers see their first risk surfaced within minutes. PPC optimization and listing improvements surface within your first week as the agents finish indexing your catalog.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You own your data completely. Export everything anytime as CSV or JSON, and we'll help you transition smoothly. No lock-in, no hostage situations. Revoke SP-API access from Seller Central at any moment.
Do I have to stop using my other tools?
Not at all. Sydon connects with Seller Central, QuickBooks, ShipStation, Stripe, Klaviyo, HubSpot, Keepa, and Google Ads. Keep your workflows; we read alongside them. Most sellers retire 3–5 redundant tools by month two — on their schedule, not ours.
Is this too advanced for smaller sellers?
Sydon scales with your business. Whether you're at $100K or $10M annually, the platform adapts. Start with the free Account Guardian, unlock more agents as the math justifies it.
What Amazon data does Sydon access through SP-API?
The same business data you see in your Seller Central Dashboard — orders, inventory, financial reports, performance metrics, and listing content. We never access buyer personal information. No changes happen without your explicit approval.
Are you compliant with Amazon's new agent policy?
Yes — Sydon was built from day one for Amazon's March 2026 Agent Policy, including the new transparency, approval, and audit-log requirements. Every agent action is logged, attributable, and reversible.
